1 /*
2  Rn model parameters version 2020/09/25
3  Author: Guillaume Pronost
4 
5  Detector slicing:
6  - The detector is divided in X layers over R2 from center to side, and Y layer over Z, from bottom to top.
7  ex: R2 layer 0 is at the center of the detector, Z layer 0 is the bottom of the detector
8  - R2 layers:
9  - For each R2 layer, we have a Z distribution
10  - There are RNMODEL_NBIN_R layers
11  - For each Z layer we have a R2 distribution
12  - Due to the noise, fit is impossible in the layers too close to the walls
13  -> R2 layers after RNMODEL_BIN_R_MAX are not fitted
14  -> Z layers below RNMODEL_BIN_Z_MIN and above RNMODEL_BIN_Z_MAX are not fitted
15 
16  Fit parameter explanations:
17  - vParam_Z[x][..] are the model fit parameters of the Z distribution from R2 layer x
18  [0]: bottom Z-limit of the convection front (unit: m)
19  [1]: upper Z-limit of the convection front (unit: m)
20  [2]: Radon concentration in the above the convection front (unit: mBq/m3)
21  [3]: Radon concentration in the below the convection front (unit: mBq/m3)
22  [4]: Radon concentration in the convection front (unit: mBq/m3)
23  [5]: Correction to the diffusion factor to the top from the convection front (unit: abr.)
24  [6]: Correction to the diffusion factor to the bottom from the convection front (unit: abr.)
25  - vParam_R2[y][..] are the model fit parameters of the R2 distribution from Z layer y
26  [0]: Constant Radon concentration on the detector side (unit: mBq/m3)
27  [1]: R2 value where fast diffusion toward the center starts (unit: m2)
28  [2]: Correction to the diffusion factor for the fast diffusion (unit: abr.)
29  Note: fast diffusion -> short range
30  [3]: Radon concentration in the center of the detector (unit: mBq/m3)
31  [4]: (Optional) R2 value where slow diffusion toward the detector center starts (unit: m2)
32  [5]: (Optional) Correction to the diffusion factor for the slow diffusion (unit: abr.)
33  [6]: (Optional) Radon contration at [4]
34  Note: slow diffusion -> long range
35 
36 */
